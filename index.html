Pseudo Markdown rules
  • '.h1 ' at beginning of line - Header
  • '- ' at beginning of line • unordered list
  • '---' entire line - hr
  • __text__ or **text** - Bold
  • --text-- - Grayed out text for minor notes

  • ~~text~~ (tilde) - for strikethru text
